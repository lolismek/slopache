# Trend research — June 2026

Source material for episode ideas. Two distinct buckets (the user's distinction):

- **Brain-rot** = dumb, absurdist, niche, meme-literate (Gen-Alpha/Z). The joke is that
  there is no joke. Smaller but rabid, share-y audience.
- **Trends** = superficial but broadly relatable lifestyle/aesthetic formats. Large general
  audience.

Each entry: what it is · recency (with source) · **grandma angle** (the influencer × Romanian
village clash). Collected via web research 2026-06-18; recency verified against sources.
Episodes drafted from this list are tagged **→ epNN**.

---

## A. Brain-rot — international

1. **"6-7" meme** — nonsensical phrase + palms-up hand gesture, Gen-Alpha in-group signal.
   Peaked Aug–Nov 2025; Dictionary.com 2025 Word of the Year. Still circulating 2026.
   `thesetonian.com` / `en.wikipedia.org/wiki/6-7`. **→ ep03** (counts eggs "six… seven").
2. **Italian brainrot** (Tralalero Tralala, Tung Tung Tung Sahur, Ballerina Cappuccina,
   Bombardiro Crocodilo) — AI animal/object fusions with fake-Italian narration. Started
   Jan 2025, alive 2026 (Fortnite skins Apr 2026). Ballerina Cappuccina was made by a
   **24-yr-old Romanian** (Susanu Sava-Tudor). `en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Italian_brainrot`.
   **→ ep04** ("Bunica Cappuccina" — real coffee vs the AI ballerina).
3. **Clanker** — Star-Wars-derived "slur" for robots/AI, ironic faux-bigotry at delivery
   bots/ChatGPT. Viral Jul–Aug 2025 (NPR, Rolling Stone). **→ ep05** (broom-chases a robot
   vacuum, "Afară, clanker!").
4. **Aura farming** — cultivating effortless "coolness" via repetitive moves; Indonesian
   boat-prow dance kid went viral mid-2025. `en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Aura_farming`.
   **→ ep07** (aura-farms on a rowboat on the village pond).
5. **Nothing beats a Jet2 holiday** — cheery ad slogan ironically over travel disasters.
   Massive Mar–summer 2025. `knowyourmeme.com`. (Unused — needs licensed audio.)
6. **Crash out / crashing out** — sudden emotional meltdown slang. Exploded summer 2025,
   still heavy 2026. `knowyourmeme.com`. (Backup idea — "I'm about to crash out" over a
   dropped jar of pickles.)
7. **Great Meme Reset of 2026** — Jan 1 2026 movement to revert memes to 2016; failed,
   became a meme. `dailydot.com`. (Backup.)
8. **June-2026 lip-sync audios** — "Melissaaaaaa I'm drunk and outside", "I'm an astronaut
   you're the moon", "Rich in life", Tung Tung Tung Sahur duet dance. `ramd.am`, `later.com`,
   `buffer.com`. (Audio-dependent — revisit when we license sounds.)
